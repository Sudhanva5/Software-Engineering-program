13 FAQ

Before you decide.

Why pay for this when free resources and AI tools exist? +

Access to information isn't the bottleneck. Sequence, feedback loops, deadlines, and project proof are. Free resources don't provide the review that catches weak judgment before an interview does.

Do I need a technical background to start? +

Not always. Beginner, intermediate, and advanced starting points are supported. A Scaler Admission Test places you correctly. The real question: are you ready for 12 months of consistent effort?

How does AI show up inside the programme? +

Throughout — not as a module at the end. DSA labs, debugging, review, system design, and a compulsory Agentic AI module. You own correctness at every stage.

Can this fit around a full-time job? +

Classes 3 days a week — 7 AM or 9 PM, 1.5–2 hours. Built for working professionals. Deadline policy covered in the live class.

What's the difference between the three tracks? +

The opening ramp differs — beginner starts with fundamentals, intermediate goes direct to DSA, advanced opens with Agentic AI. All tracks converge at the same standard.

What should I expect by the end of the year? +

Stronger fundamentals, showcase projects, AI workflows you can defend, and sharper interview communication. Specifics depend on your track and starting point.
